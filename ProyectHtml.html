Restaurant Menu

  • Portuguese Codfish

    Delicious codfish prepared following the traditional Portuguese recipe. Served with steamed potatoes and fresh vegetables.

  • Francesinha

    A unique combination of sausages, meat, cheese, and a special sauce, served with French fries.

  • Custard Tart

    A traditional Portuguese dessert made with a crispy pastry and a filling of egg custard and vanilla.

  • Duck Rice

    A traditional Portuguese duck rice dish, slowly cooked with herbs and spices for a rich and flavorful taste.

  • Green Soup (Caldo Verde)

    A comforting green soup made with collard greens, potatoes, and Portuguese chorizo. Perfect for any time of the year.

  • Pork with Clams (Carne de Porco à Alentejana)

    A popular dish of marinated pork with clams, served with French fries and olives. A delight for meat lovers!

Our History

From our humble beginnings as a small café in the heart of Lisbon, we have grown to become a culinary destination known for our authentic Portuguese cuisine and exceptional hospitality.
